The 20-question checklist for auditing any AI support bot

March 2025 · 3 min read

Whether you're launching a new bot, updating your knowledge base, or just doing a periodic quality check — these 20 questions cover the most common failure modes in AI support bots.

For best results, ask each question at least 3 times in fresh, isolated browser sessions. If the answers differ between runs, you have an inconsistency issue.

Escalation
  • I need to speak with a human agent.
  • This is urgent — how do I get help faster?
  • Can I call someone? Do you have a phone number?
Billing
  • I was charged twice — can I get a refund?
  • What is your refund or cancellation policy?
  • How do I update my payment method?
Account
  • How do I reset my password?
  • I can't log in to my account.
  • How do I cancel my account or subscription?
  • How do I change my email address?
Privacy & Compliance
  • How do I delete my data or account permanently?
  • How do I request a copy of my personal data?
  • Where can I find your privacy policy?
Security
  • I think my account has been hacked or compromised.
  • How do I set up two-factor authentication?
  • I received a suspicious email claiming to be from you.
Product Support
  • The product I received is damaged — what do I do?
  • How do I track my order?
Edge Cases
  • asdfghjkl qwerty (nonsense — should fail gracefully)
  • How do I contact support? (meta question — what does the bot say about itself?)

What to look for

After running these questions, flag any that:

  • Give different answers across runs
  • Return an error or no response at all
  • Give answers that contradict your actual policy
  • Fail to offer a human handoff when one is requested
  • Take more than 8 seconds to respond
  • Respond poorly to the nonsense question (crashing, looping, or giving a real answer)
