DESCRIPTION (shown on product page)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Most teams configure their support bot and never test it again.
BotAudit does.

You give it a URL and a set of questions. It opens your live support
bot in a real browser, sends each question multiple times in fresh
sessions, and checks whether the answers are consistent.

The result: a full PDF audit report showing every response,
every inconsistency, every error, and response times — ready to
share with your team or use to fix your bot configuration.

→ No setup or account required
→ Works with Zendesk Messaging and other popular chat platforms
→ Free 3-question demo, then pay once per audit (no subscription)
→ Results in 5–10 minutes

Hey PH! 👋

I built BotAudit after noticing that no one was systematically
testing their support bots.

We all know that code needs tests. We do QA on our apps. But the
bot that handles thousands of customer conversations per month?
Most teams test it once, manually, and then ship it.

The problem is subtle: support bots can give *inconsistent* answers.
Ask the same question three times, get three slightly different
responses. That's not a failure you'd notice in manual testing —
you'd just see "it answered." But your customers notice over time,
and it quietly erodes their trust.

BotAudit was born out of frustration with that. I wanted a tool
that would:

1. Ask each question multiple times (not just once)
2. Test in fresh browser sessions (no memory between runs)
3. Flag inconsistencies automatically
4. Generate a shareable PDF so I could actually show someone
   what the problem was

Happy to answer any questions about how it works or what we're
planning next. Go test your support bot! 🔍
